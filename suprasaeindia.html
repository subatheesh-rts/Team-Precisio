SUPRA SAEINDIA
About Supra
SUPRA is an event that challenges young minds to compete as teams in designing a Formula category vehicle, there is no other event like this in the country. It is organised and conducted by the Society of Automotive Engineers aka SAE. It is an annual event, going to be held in Buddh International Circuit, Delhi this year.
